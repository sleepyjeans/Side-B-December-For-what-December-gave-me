Letters Written in Winter

something warm, just for you

December 01

There’s a version of winter that isn’t cold. It sounds like your voice in my head and feels like knowing someone understands me without asking.

I think of you when the world slows down. When the lights are low. When everything feels honest.

December 02

I'm sure that I'll melt into your touch the moment I feel it, like gravity will forget about me. I'm sure it'll feel like weightless and make me float up to the sky, and I'm sure it'll heal me of any illness that exists. I think that's your superpower.

Thinking of you tonight.

Yours truly, with love.
